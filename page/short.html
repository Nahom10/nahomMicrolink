Comp TIA Certifications

The Computing Technology Industry Association (CompTIA) is the voice of the world’s information technology (IT) industry. It is dedicated to advancing industry growth through convergence technology, e-commerce, IT training, software services, certification, public policy and workforce development. Comp TIA serves the IT industry as the world’s largest developer of vendor- neutral IT certification exams. Experts and industry leaders provide the resources and subject matter expertise necessary to build a vendor-neutral industry- defined certification exam. To date, more than one million CompTIA certifications have been earned worldwide. At the moment, Micro like is delivering A+, Network +, Security+, & Project+ trainings at its Giorgis branch.

A+

CompTIA A+ certification validates the latest skills needed by today’s computer support professionals. It is an international, vendor- neutral certification recognized by major hardware and software vendors, distributors and resellers. CompTIA A+ certification confirms a technician‘s ability to perform tasks such as installation, configuration, diagnosing, preventive maintenance and basic networking. Comp TIA A+ is composed of four courses Comp TIA A+ Essentials, Comp TIA A+ 220-602, Comp TIA A+ 220-603, & Comp TIA A+ 220-604.

Network +

CompTIA Network + validates the knowledge and skills of network professionals. It is an international, vendor-neutral certification that recognizes a technician’s ability to describe the features and functions of networking components and to manage, maintain, troubleshoot, install, operate and configure basic network infrastructure.

Security+

CompTIA Security+ validates knowledge of systems security, network infrastructure, access control, assessments and audits, cryptography and organizational security. It is and international, vendor -neutral security certification that is taught at colleges, universities and commercial training centers around the world. CompTIA Security+ is recognized by the technology community as a voluble credential that proves competency with information security.

Project+

CompTIA project+ is a globally recognized project management certification that provides validation of fundamental project management skills. It covers the entire project life cycle from initiation and planning through execution, acceptance, support and close. Comp TIA project + gives project managers the skills necessary to complete project management language among project team members.
